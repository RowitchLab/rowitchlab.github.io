Research Focus

Investigating the Genetic Basis of Glial Cell Development and Neurological Diseases

Glial Cell Development

Studying the genetic factors that determine the development and diversity of glial cells, which comprise 90% of cells in the human brain.

  • • Oligodendrocyte lineage specification
  • • Astrocyte functional diversification
  • • Microglia origins and functions

White Matter Diseases

Applying developmental neuroscience principles to understand and treat white matter injuries and diseases.

  • • Multiple sclerosis
  • • Leukodystrophies (Pelizaeus-Merzbacher)
  • • Premature infant brain injury

Precision Medicine

Developing genomic technologies for diagnosis and treatment of rare neurogenetic disorders in children.

  • • Whole-genome sequencing applications
  • • Predictive genomics in pediatrics
  • • Gene therapy development

Pediatric Neuroscience

Focusing on the unique aspects of brain development and neurological disorders in children.

  • • Cerebral palsy genetics
  • • Neonatal brain development
  • • Pediatric neuro-oncology

Stem Cell Therapy

Leading clinical trials for neural stem cell transplantation in neurological disorders.

  • • Phase I clinical studies
  • • Neural stem cell engraftment
  • • Myelination restoration

Advanced Technologies

Utilizing cutting-edge technologies to advance neuroscience research.

  • • Single-cell RNA sequencing
  • • Spatial transcriptomics
  • • In vivo imaging techniques
