Our Research

We focus on several key research areas, leveraging interdisciplinary approaches to address complex scientific questions.

Computational Biology

Developing algorithms and computational models to understand biological systems at multiple levels of organization, from molecules to entire organisms.

Learn more

Bioinformatics

Applying computational techniques to analyze and interpret biological data, with a focus on genomics, proteomics, and systems biology approaches.

Learn more

Systems Biology

Studying the complex interactions within biological systems to understand emergent properties and develop predictive models of biological behavior.

Learn more

Structural Biology

Investigating the three-dimensional structures of biological macromolecules and their complexes to understand their functions and interactions.

Learn more
