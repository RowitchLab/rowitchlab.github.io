OUR RESEARCH

Investigating
Molecular Mechanisms

Our laboratory focuses on understanding the fundamental mechanisms of chromosome organization and RNA regulation. We employ advanced single-molecule techniques and genomic approaches to address these questions.

  • Single-molecule tracking in live cells
  • Chromosome structure and dynamics
  • RNA processing and regulation
  • Super-resolution microscopy
